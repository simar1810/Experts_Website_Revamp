## **1\. WHAT ARE COOKIES?**

Cookies are small text files placed on your browser, device, or system when you visit or use a website or digital platform. They help websites and applications remember your actions, preferences, session details, and technical information.

We may also use similar technologies such as:

- pixels,

- SDKs,

- local storage,

- device identifiers,

- tags,

- scripts,

- and analytics tools,

for purposes similar to cookies.

For simplicity, all such technologies are referred to in this Policy as “**Cookies**”.

---

## **2\. WHY WE USE COOKIES**

We use Cookies to help operate, secure, analyze, and improve the Platform. Depending on the feature, Cookies may be used for:

- keeping you signed in;

- authenticating your account or session;

- remembering preferences and settings;

- improving website and app performance;

- understanding how users interact with the Platform;

- measuring traffic and usage trends;

- personalizing content or user experience;

- supporting security, fraud prevention, and abuse detection;

- helping with marketing and campaign performance measurement.

CERT-In’s security guidance and related incident-response expectations also make it important for digital platforms to maintain logging, monitoring, and security controls, which in practice often works alongside session and device-level technologies.
